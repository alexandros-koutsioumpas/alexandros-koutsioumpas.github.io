New "juicy" article[1] making heavy use of neutron reflectometry showing the importance of leaflet assymetry in biomimetic lipid membranes in relation to Myelin protein binding. Very patient and detailed work by out colleuague Dr. Julio Pusterla on the preparation of supported assymetric membranes for these experiments. Also happy to see [Anaklasis](https://github.com/alexandros-koutsioumpas/anaklasis) beeing actively used for data treatment!
